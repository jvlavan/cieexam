The eighth-generation Porsche 911 has arrived in India in two variants - 911 Carrera S and the 911 Carerra S Cabriolet. The new 911, which is internally codenamed as 992 gets the distinctive 911 family face. The vehicle is based on the new modular MMB platform which will also underpins the next generation of 718 Cayman and 718 Boxster models.
